Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a personal shopping appointment?

You can schedule a personal shopping appointment by calling our concierge team at +1 (800) 555-LUXE, booking online through our appointment system, or visiting any of our boutiques in person. Our personal shopping experiences typically last 90 minutes and include champagne service.

Do you offer alterations on clothing purchases?

Yes, we provide complimentary basic alterations on all ready-to-wear purchases. More complex alterations may incur additional fees. Our in-house tailors typically complete alterations within 3-5 business days. For couture pieces, we include up to three fittings in the purchase price.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for store credit or exchange. All limited edition and made-to-order items are final sale. Returns must be in original condition with all tags attached. For boutique purchases, please bring your receipt for faster processing.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. For international orders, we'll also provide customs documentation. If you have an account with us, you can view all order details and tracking information in your profile. For urgent inquiries, our concierge team can provide updates.
