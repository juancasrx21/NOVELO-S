Redefining Luxury Since 2010

Novelo Luxe was born from a vision to create timeless pieces that blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.

Our journey began in a small Milanese atelier and has grown into an international luxury house, while maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and artisanal excellence.

Our Milestones

2010

Sustainable Luxury

True luxury respects both people and planet.

We're pioneering a new standard in responsible luxury through:

  • Ethically sourced materials: 100% traceable supply chain
  • Carbon-neutral shipping: Offset through reforestation projects
  • Timeless designs: Created to last beyond seasons
  • Lifetime care: Repair and restoration services
  • Circular program: Buy-back and recycling initiative
